Weather Data API

URL format: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/station/date

For getting weather of a particular day: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/10/1988-10-25

For getting weather for all days at one station: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/12

For getting weather of an entire year: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/yearly/12/1988
